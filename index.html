H&M Policies

Our policies

We have a broad set of various policies in place to guide us in our sustainability work. These group-wide policies complement our strong corporate values, which state that we want to act sustainably in everything we do. Animal Welfare Policy Our Animal Welfare Policy outlines our responsibility to secure our supply of raw materials from an animal welfare perspective through tough requirements and full traceability.

Chemical Restrictions

Our Chemicals Restrictions are as a minimum always based on the highest legal standard in any of our sales countries as well as on information from authorities, NGOs and scientific reports. Our restriction list is one of the most stringent in the apparel industry. (Available in English only) Child Labour Policy

Code of Ethics

H&M group does not accept child labour. This is clearly stated in our Sustainability Commitment for business partners, as well as in this Child labour policy, which also outlines our expectations on our business partners should child labour be found.

Global Labour Relations Policy

At the H&M group, we make it a rule to act with integrity at all times. Our Code of Ethics outlines our requirements towards employees and suppliers.
